Valorant Match Tracker

This is a Discord bot that tracks your match scores in your Discord server!

Invite the bot to your server by clicking the link below:

Invite the bot

Once the bot is invited, you can use the following commands:

  • /link: Link your Riot account to enable match tracking.
  • /set_tracker: Set a tracker in a specific channel for a linked player.
  • /stats_update: Manually update the stats of the selected player. After this, the stats will automatically update.
