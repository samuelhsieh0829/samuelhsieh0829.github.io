Valorant Match Tracker

This is a Discord bot, which can track your match score on your discord server :)

Invite the bot to your server by clicking the link below:

Invite the bot

After inviting the bot, you can use the following commands:

  • /link to link your Riot account in order to become tracked-able
  • /set_tracker to set a tracker in the specific channel and player for a linked player
  • /stats_update to update the stats of the selected player, after that the stats will be automatically update
